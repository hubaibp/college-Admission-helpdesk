Eligibility

1. Girl students

2. For Muslim/Latin/Converted christian community

3. Minimum of 50% mark in 12th Examination

4. Annual family income less than 8 lakhs

Central Sector Scholarship
Eligibility

1. Students who are above 80th percentile of successful candidates in the relevant stream from a particular Board of Examination in Class XII

2. Pursuing regular course

3. Having family income of less than Rs.6 lakh per annum

4. Not receiving any other scholarship

Pragati scholarship
Eligibility

1. Only girl students can apply for this scholarship

2. The girl candidate must be studying in an AICTE approved college/institution

3. Applicants must be admitted to the 1st-year of the Diploma/Degree course of an AICTE approved college/institution in the current academic year

4. The annual income of the family must not be more than INR 8 Lakh. For married applicants, the income of parents/in-law is considered, whichever is higher

Vidya Samunathi scholarship
Eligibility

1. Applicants should be from non-reservation forward community (General) and a resident of Kerala

2. Annual income of the family should be less than 2 lakhs based on all sources

3. Applicants must have a Bank account in any recognised bank

Department

CS

 

Department of Computer Science and Engineering was established in the year 1993 and in the same year itself department has startedoffering 4 year B.Tech programme in Computer Science and Engineering with an intake of 60. The major strength of the department is highly qualified and experienced faculty with specialization in main areas of computer science and engineering and information technology.

MECH

 

Department of Mechanical Engineering offers 4 year B.Tech degree course in Mechanical engineering and M.Tech course in Thermal and fluid power Engineering. B.Tech in Mechanical Engineering was one among the 4 courses started when the college was established in 1993. P.G Course started in the year 2012. The Department has recorded a consistent growth over the years.

EEE

 

The department is well stabilized with vibrant faculty members and well setup laboratories. Highly qualified staff and skilled supporting staff, whom are dedicated to work are strength of department. The staff members are involved day to day college activities and carries out consultancy works in electrical field with active involvement from students.

EC

 

The Department was established in the year 1993 with B.Tech programme in Electronics and Communication Engineering with a mission to enable the students to develop into outstanding professionals with high ethical standards to face the challenges of future, and to fulfill the expectation of our society by equipping our students to stride forth as successful citizens to make a better world.

IT

 

Department of IT offers undergraduate programmes in Information Technology andMaster of Computer Application programme.The effective use of IT is a major component of economic growth and innovation.Since its inception in the year 2000, the department has progressed rapidly to keep pace withthe needs of the time and is now regarded as one of the premiere departments.

CIVIL

 

The Department of Civil Engineering pulls out all halts to mould outstanding Engineers. The Civil Engineering discipline has been offered with annual intake of 60 at LBS college of engineering Kasaragod right from 2008. The department has an outstanding track record and has consistently produce CivilEngineering Graduates of exceptional quality and calibre.
